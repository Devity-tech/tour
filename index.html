About Macclesfield

Macclesfield is a historic market town in Cheshire, England, known for its silk heritage and stunning countryside views.

Johnny Depp has chosen this intimate venue for a special performance that will feature music from his career along with personal storytelling.

The Macclesfield Heritage Centre provides an ideal setting for this unique event, with excellent acoustics and a capacity that ensures an intimate experience for all attendees.

For those traveling to Macclesfield, the town offers excellent transport links, quality accommodation, and a variety of dining options to make your visit memorable.

Venue Location

Macclesfield Heritage Centre, Macclesfield SK10 1EA, UK

The venue is easily accessible by train (Macclesfield Station) and by car via the A523.
